Introduction

Welcome to Spell Combos. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy notice will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights.

Data We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide our service:

  • Username and password (if you register)
  • IP address hashes (for voting and reporting)
  • Content you submit (combos, votes, reports)

We do not collect any other personal information. IP addresses are stored as hashes, not in their original form.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Prevent duplicate votes and reports
  • Attribute content to creators
  • Improve our service

Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Account information: Until you delete your account
  • Votes: 30 days (automatically deleted after this period)
  • Combos and reports: Until deleted by moderation or by you

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of your personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to processing of your personal data
